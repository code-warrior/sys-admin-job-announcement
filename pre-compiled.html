CS Dept Seeks Student System Administrators

The Dept of Computing Sciences seeks candidates to join our team of sys admins, currently consisting of two students and a faculty supervisor. Sys admins maintain hardware and software systems used by over 100 faculty, staff, and students — on and off campus. Two positions are available for immediate occupancy.

qualifications

Candidates should possess at least the following required qualifications, with preference given to those with preferred experience.

Required

  • cs, mwd, or cse major
  • First-year or sophomore standing, as of spring 2021
  • Plan to graduate from uhart
  • Good analytical writing skills
  • Laser-focused task commitment in order to meet given deadlines
  • Neat, organized, and detail-oriented
  • Ability to work extended and irregular hours when the need arises
  • Some level of passion for technology
  • A curious nature to solve problems, even when not possessing all the knowledge required to do so
  • Effective team collaboration skills

Preferred

  • Some experience working inside computers, such as to upgrade ram, exchange power supply units, or switch out storage devices
  • Any experience installing and/or configuring servers
  • Some skill with Linux/unix
  • Involvement with network management, either in Windows, Linux, or both
  • Some experience in writing bash scripts
  • Skill in working with Markdown syntax
  • Experience filing issues in Trello, ZenHub, JIRA, or other kanban-like systems
  • Project experience working with Git, including branching, forking, cloning, and pull requests
  • Have prepared and/or maintained CAT 5 network cables
